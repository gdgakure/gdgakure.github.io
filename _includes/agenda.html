Agenda

Day 1: Design Sprint

This day is going to feature Group brainstorming, where everyone shouts out ideas - it is a lot of fun. At the end of a workshop we’d be tired, in good spirits, and the proud owners of a big pile of sticky notes. We would be taking a cue from http://gv.com/designsprint We would be doing exactly what the industry leader “Google” does when coming up with a new Product.

Day 2: Polymer Polytechnic Hackathon

We plan this hack day to be a format for understanding Polymer in depth because you have time set aside to actually code while still within reach of support. It's a great environment to take an existing app and convert pieces over to Polymer elements, or start on a new app entirely. We recommend that all participants read through the Polymer Basics guide , though they can skip over the initial steps covered in “Getting the Code” since they’ll be doing that during the codelabs. Instead, we encourage everyone to try out the interactive samples by clicking the “Edit on Plunker” buttons. That will be the fastest way to start hacking on Polymer.
